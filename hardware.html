First you should know what a motherboard is, It is a printed circuit board which has the main components of a computer or other device. .


    Central Processing Unit (CPU)

    Another thing that is important to know is what the CPU is. It is the abbreviation for central processing unit and it basically is the brains of the computer.

    Random Access Memory (RAM)

    Another key term would be RAM. RAM is the acronym for Random Access Memory and it is a type of computer memory that can be accessed randomly and it is the most common type of memory found in computers.

    Hard Drive

    The hard drive of your computer is a high capacity, self-contained storage device on your computer. It basically retrieves and stores information you saved.

    These components all make up your computer and they are important to understand for coding.
